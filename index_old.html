Hi Guys!

Couldn’t resist scrolling down on us huh ?  

We are more than happy to introduce ourselves to you!

We, Kwality are the 1st (obviously   ) ice cream manufacturers in gulf.

We have celebrated 40 years in the Middle East and have been creating delicious moments in the lives of people (as delicious as our ice creams   ) with presence in more than 20 countries!

In 2011, we expanded into foods category by launching paneer, frozen vegetables & finger food. We have since expanded into rice and beverages!

Kwality is now a household name in UAE and has managed to win the hearts of Indians and non Indians alike with its heritage range of products!
