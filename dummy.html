About Us We Just Love Kwality

Hi Guys! Couldn’t resist scrolling down on us huh ;)

We are more than happy to introduce ourselves to you!

We, Kwality are one of the first few ice cream brands of India and have been around since the 1940’s (Loooong time :D)

In order to expand, we, Kwality ice creams, established ourselves in UAE as Pure Ice Cream Co. L.L.C (Because we really are pure ice creams, isn’t it? :P ) in 1977 hence becoming the first ever brand to pioneer ice-cream manufacturing in the Middle East (Always the first ;) )

In 2011, we expanded into foods category by establishing Pure Canadian Foods and launched paneer, frozen vegetables & finger food. We have since expanded into rice and beverages!

Kwality is now a household name in UAE and has managed to win the hearts of Indians and non Indians alike with its heritage range of products!
